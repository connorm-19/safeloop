Why cost share only?

In British Columbia, a ride counts as a carpool, not a private driver-for-hire, only when the driver recovers actual trip costs and nothing more.

That means a driver can be reimbursed for fuel, insurance, and parking attributable to the trip, but never a profit or a fare. Charging above cost would make it a commercial passenger service, which requires a special licence.

SafeLoop keeps every amount at or below true cost share so your circle stays a legal carpool.

Safety

Safety rules & guidelines

Review the core rules that keep children, parents, and rides safe inside SafeLoop.

Verified parents only

✓Only verified parents can request, offer, or claim rides.
✓Driver licence, insurance, and payment details must remain active.

Pickup and drop-off rules

✓Always confirm the child identity, pickup note, and destination before departure.
✓Children should only be released to the assigned verified driver.

During the ride

✓Use seatbelts and follow BC child safety and booster seat requirements.
✓Keep live tracking on whenever a ride is active.
✓Contact the parent immediately if pickup timing or route changes.

Cancellations and emergencies

✓Cancel as early as possible so the circle has time to reassign the ride.
✓If an emergency happens, use the parent contact tools first, then call local emergency services if needed.
Need more information? Contact SafeLoop Support at support@safeloop.ca or call 1-800-555-LOOP.
